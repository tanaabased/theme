# Principles

<TMSSection>

    precise, quiet, and easy to read.
  </p>

<TMSSection border-top>
  <template #title>

  </template>
  <TMSGrid
    columns="4"

    <TMSBox type="title">Clear</TMSBox>
    <TMSBox type="title">Brief</TMSBox>
    <TMSBox type="title">Human</TMSBox>
    <TMSBox type="title">Wry</TMSBox>
  </TMSGrid>
  <p>
    Say useful things clearly and quickly, keep the human operator visible, and
    avoid letting the copy collapse into anonymous platform language.
  </p>
</TMSSection>
