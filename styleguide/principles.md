  <p>
    The system is serious, but self-aware enough not to become self-important.
  </p>
  <p>
    Hard edges lead; soft shapes punctuate the system.
  </p>
  <p>
    Pop-culture asides, snark, and the occasional
    <a class="tms-hidden-link" href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tkBVDh7my9Q" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">hidden link</a>
    are allowed when they earn the interruption.
  </p>
  <p>
    For example, <strong>non-critical copy</strong> can walk the line between
    seriousness and irony in roughly the same way The Darkness does in
    <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tKjZuykKY1I" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer"><em>I Believe in a Thing Called Love</em></a>:
    fully committed, technically competent, and aware of its own theater.
  </p>
</TMSSection>
